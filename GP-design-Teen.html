Diet Suggestions For Teenagers: Overwiew

Tennagers refers to the young people from the age 12 to 17. During this crucial period of time both the male and the female body experiences a lot of physical changes and it is essential to follow a healthy diet for teenagers in order to support the changes. In particular, teenagers need extra calcium and vitamin D for bone growth, and protein for muscle development. Therefore, a balanced diet for teenagers need to consist sufficient protein, carbohydrates, minerals, vitamins and healthy fats.

A teenage boy requires 2600 calories per day, while a teenage girl requires 2400 calories per day. The demand of those nutrients from boys and girls are slightly different, so in this article we give advices for boys and girls separately.

Examples of some protein rich foods: red meat, fish, pulses and cereal

Examples of some calcium rich foods: milk, cheese, tofu and nuts

Examples of some vitamin D rich foods: breakfast cereals and oily fishes
