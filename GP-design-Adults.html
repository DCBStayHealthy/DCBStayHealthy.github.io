Diet Suggestions For Adults: Overwiew

This article is aimed at people who are between 18 and 65 years old. In general, during this period of time, the calorie consumption is the greatest amoung all age groups because the the level of both physical and mental activity is the highest.

Adults need to consume enough proteins and carbohydrates to make sure the body has enough "fuel" supplied for a day's hard work. Otherwise you might feel tired or unable to concentrate due to lack of nutrition.

A Sample Diet For Women

Breakfast

  • Banana smoothie made with 1 banana

  • 1 cup of skim milk

  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter

  • 2 scrambled eggs

  • 1 slice of whole grain bread

    • Mid-Morning Snack

  • 10 whole grain crackers

  • 2 tablespoons of hummus

    • Lunch

  • 1 cup of cooked brown rice

  • 85 grams of grilled or baked fish

  • 1 baked sweet potato

  • 1 cup spinach salad

  • 2 small oat cookies

    • Evening Snack

  • 1/2 cup of cottage cheese with 2 tablespoons of almonds

  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

    • Dinner

  • Pasta salad made with 1 cup whole wheat pasta

  • 1/2 cup chicken breast diced

  • chopped bell peppers and tomatoes

  • 1 Pear
